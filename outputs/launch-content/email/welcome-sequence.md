Preview text: Eight arms, one kind heart.

Body:

CTA: Explore the Character Universe

## Email 4: The Power of Kindness

Subject: A simple way to teach kindness through stories

Preview text: Picture books make big feelings easier to talk about.

Body:

Stories help children practice kindness before they need it in real life.

When Ollie helps a friend, slows down, says sorry, shares, or tries again, children get a safe little model they can remember later.

Try this after reading:

"Who helped in the story?"

"How did they know a friend needed help?"

"What could we do if that happened at home or school?"

Small questions like these turn storytime into a gentle social-emotional learning moment.
