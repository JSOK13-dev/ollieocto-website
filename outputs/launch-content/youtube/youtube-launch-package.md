# YouTube Launch Package

## Channel Description

Welcome to Ollie the Octopus, a parent-friendly children's story channel set in Sparkle Bay.

Join Ollie, Stella Starfish, Spike the Pufferfish, Zippy the Seahorse, Grumpy Gus the Lobster, Lily the Sea Turtle, Keke the Endangered Elkhorn Coral, and the magical Rainbow Bubble for ocean adventures about friendship, teamwork, kindness, courage, and gentle preschool learning.

Perfect for ages 3-5, read-aloud time, bedtime, classroom storytime, and families who love ocean animals.

Kind Hearts. Big Adventures.

Every Adventure Begins with a Bubble.

## Video 1 Script: Meet Ollie the Octopus

Hook: Have you ever met an octopus with eight helping arms?

Narration:

Meet Ollie the Octopus. Ollie lives in Sparkle Bay, where the water glows, the coral sparkles, and every adventure begins with a bubble.

Ollie is curious, kind, and always ready to help a friend. His superpower is his eight arms. One arm can wave hello. One arm can hold a shell. One arm can help a friend who is stuck. And together, all eight arms can solve big Sparkle Bay problems.

Ollie's catchphrase is, "Let's see where the adventure takes us!"

Question for kids: What would you do with eight helping arms?

CTA: Ask a grown-up to subscribe for more Sparkle Bay adventures.

## Video 2 Script: The Great Bubble Chase

Hook: Look! The Rainbow Bubble!

Narration:

One bright morning in Sparkle Bay, Ollie peeked outside and saw something amazing. A giant Rainbow Bubble floated right past his door.

"Let's catch it!" Ollie said.

Stella joined. Then more friends joined. The bubble floated through coral, around shells, and into the Kelp Forest.

Uh-oh. The friends got tangled.

Ollie stretched his eight arms wide and gently pulled the kelp apart. Everyone was free.

With friends by your side, every adventure turns out just right.

CTA: Read Ollie the Octopus and the Great Bubble Chase.

## Video 3 Script: Meet Stella Starfish

This is Stella Starfish, Ollie's best friend.

Stella is optimistic and encouraging. When something feels tricky, Stella looks for a bright idea.

Her superpower is seeing the bright side.

Her catchphrase is, "There's always a way to shine!"

When Ollie feels unsure, Stella cheers him on. When a friend feels left out, Stella helps them feel welcome.

## Video 4 Script: Meet Spike the Pufferfish

Hook: Can you be brave and scared at the same time?

Narration:

Meet Spike the Pufferfish.

Spike feels nervous sometimes. New places, big bubbles, and tangled kelp can make him puff up.

But Spike has a special kind of courage. He tries even when he feels scared.

His catchphrase is, "I can do hard things!"

Spike reminds kids that brave does not mean never feeling nervous. Brave means taking one little step.

CTA: Try saying it with Spike: I can do hard things!
