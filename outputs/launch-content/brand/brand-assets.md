# Ollie the Octopus Brand Assets

## Brand Message

Core message: Kind Hearts. Big Adventures.

Secondary tagline: Every Adventure Begins with a Bubble.

Setting: Sparkle Bay

Book title: Ollie the Octopus and the Great Bubble Chase

## Character Bios and Descriptions

### Ollie the Octopus

Role: Main character

Trait: Curious, kind, adventurous

Superpower: Eight arms that help solve problems

Catchphrase: "Let's see where the adventure takes us!"

Description: Ollie leads each adventure with curiosity and kindness. He is the emotional center of Sparkle Bay and models teamwork, problem-solving, and helping friends.

Palette: soft blue, white, pink, purple, dark navy

Personality guide: Warm, curious, brave in a gentle way, eager to include others.

### Stella Starfish

Role: Best friend

Trait: Optimistic and encouraging

Superpower: Always sees the bright side

Catchphrase: "There's always a way to shine!"

Description: Stella helps friends notice hope, possibility, and confidence when an adventure gets tricky.

Palette: sunny yellow, warm orange, soft coral, white

Personality guide: Encouraging, cheerful, emotionally steady, supportive.

### Spike the Pufferfish

Role: Courage character

Trait: Nervous but brave

Superpower: Faces fears even when scared

Catchphrase: "I can do hard things!"

Description: Spike gives children a safe model for anxiety, courage, and trying again.

Palette: green, white, purple, dark navy

Personality guide: Cautious, honest, brave, lovable, expressive.

### Zippy the Seahorse

Role: Explorer and messenger

Trait: Fast, energetic, excited

Superpower: Speed and quick thinking

Catchphrase: "Last one there is a jellyfish!"

Description: Zippy brings movement, energy, urgency, and playful discovery to the crew.

Palette: red, white, warm tan, coral

Personality guide: Fast, funny, eager, impulsive, helpful.

### Grumpy Gus the Lobster

Role: Comic relief

Trait: Complains at first but has a big heart

Superpower: Always shows up when friends need help

Catchphrase: "I don't like adventures... but I'll help anyway."

Description: Gus gives the brand humor and emotional contrast. He grumbles, but his actions reveal kindness.

Palette: white, golden yellow, red outline, orange accents

Personality guide: Grumbly, loyal, funny, secretly tender.

### Lily the Sea Turtle

Role: Wise friend

Trait: Calm, patient, thoughtful

Superpower: Helps everyone slow down and think

Catchphrase: "Slow and steady helps us see the way."

Description: Lily brings calm problem-solving, patience, and thoughtful pauses to Sparkle Bay.

Palette: seafoam, green, aqua, dark navy

Personality guide: Gentle, wise, observant, reassuring.

### Keke the Endangered Elkhorn Coral

Role: Environmental hero

Trait: Caring, protective

Purpose: Teaches ocean conservation naturally

Catchphrase: "Every reef needs a friend."

Description: Keke gives the brand a natural bridge to ocean conservation, reef care, and classroom-friendly environmental learning.

Palette: white, deep purple, pink, orange, green bubbles

Personality guide: Caring, protective, proud of the reef, educational without sounding formal.

### The Rainbow Bubble

Role: Adventure starter

Trait: Magical mystery

Superpower: Begins each episode and points the crew toward the day's adventure

Catchphrase: "Look! The Rainbow Bubble!"

Description: The Rainbow Bubble is the repeatable story trigger for the universe. It creates familiarity, anticipation, and a clear episode structure.

Palette: aqua, coral, yellow, soft purple, white shimmer

Personality guide: Magical, surprising, gentle, joyful, mysterious.
